Tomato Sauce

Description

A delicious recipe to make your tastebuds melt! This recipe involves the work of chef Mikael. No wonder why the recipe does not have a scarcity of canola oil present.

Listed below are the ingredients for this fine dish. Hope you devour it. As you can tell, I love this dish!

Ingredients

  • Onion and Garlic
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook Onions and Garlic
  2. Add Tomatoes and Spices
  3. Stir In Cream and Butter
